💡 Tips for Farmers

Simple steps to sell more and earn more

📸

Add a Photo

Listings with a clear photo get 3 times more buyers. Use morning sunlight and place the crop on a clean surface.

📅

List on Harvest Day

Fresh listings appear higher in search. List your crop the same day you harvest to reach the most buyers.

📞

Add Your Phone Number

Buyers contact you on WhatsApp. Without a number, buyers cannot reach you.

🔴

Red Badge — Act Fast

A red badge means your crop expires in 1–2 days. Lower your price and call nearby buyers immediately.
